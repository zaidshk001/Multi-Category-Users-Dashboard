# Multi-Category-Users-Dashboard
This dashboard gives a clear view of the different categories in your business and the users who interact with them. It also shows how often users engage with one category, two categories, or multiple categories together, so you can quickly understand cross-category behavior.

Tracking multi-category users matters because it helps you see what categories are commonly used together, where overlap exists, and which combinations may have stronger demand. That kind of view is useful for planning bundles, improving recommendations, and spotting category relationships that are easy to miss in raw data.
